FOR AN AMAZING ADVENTURE

TANDEM FLIGHTS

The Canary Island offers optimal weather conditions for a tandem paragliding flight. Viewed from land Lanzarote's bio diversity is impressive. With black-lava mountains, lush vegetation, white villages, and pristine coral reefs and beaches. Experienced from the air its an awesome inspiring and unforgettable adventure. Like a bird, you will glide on the Laminar Sea coastal winds. Cruising over 15 km long beaches of Riscos de Famara and 600 meters above the lava island cliffs. Certain conditions can even accelerate flight over the gorgeous neighboring island La Graciosa. Wind directions can also grant flight to Orzola or the Mirador del Rio. Here you can land in paradise, relax on the beach and rejuvenate your soul in the crystal clear ocean waters. Once you have arrived at the starting point we will begin our detailed preparation. Once safety points and weather conditions are approved and the flight is classified as "safe" by the pilot, it is only a few steps to the ultimate adventure of the skies.

We typically take off into the wind of the Canary Islands, just a few steps together at the launch site and we gracefully fly like a bird.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DO YOU HAVE ANY DOUBT?

HOW DO I GET A GIFT VOUCHER

First we need to know the full name and the flight time of the ecipient. As soon as we have these information, we will send you the gift voucher for a paragliding tandem flight as a PDF. This can then be paid via Paypal, Revolut, Bizum or credit card.

WHERE IS THE MEETING POINT?

The meeting places depending on the weather conditions. Our team will discuss this with you in advance. However, there may be changes if the weather conditions change.

WHERE DO WE TAKE-OFF FROM?

The take-off places depending on the weather conditions and can be changed by our pilots at any time.

WHERE WILL I LAND?

Normally you will land at the same place from where you took off but the landing zone also depends on the wind and tides of the day. The pilot always has the final judgement on the safest landing based on the flying conditions.

ARE YOUR PILOTS QUALIFIED?

Yes, all our pilots are certified tandem pilots from their national certifying body and have many years of experience.

DO I NEED TO BRING ANYTHING WITH ME?

We recommend wearing footwear you can easily run a few steps in (and that won't fall off once airborne), warm close like a windbreaker, a small bottle of water and maybe sun protection. All other paragliding equipment is provided by us.

CAN I BRING MY BAG(S) WITH ME?

It's best to leave any big bags at your accommodation or in a safe place / your car. There is a compartment for small bags and day packs in the harness but we prefer you to come with as little luggage as possible.

CAN SOMEONE WHO IS NOT TAKING A TANDEM FLIGHT COME TO THE TAKE-OFF?

Anyone is welcome!

DO YOU OFFER A GROUP DISCOUNT?

We offer group pricing for groups of 6 or more. Please contact us for group pricing.

HOW DO I PAY?

You can pay via Paypal, Revolut, Bizum, Card or cach. You can pay right after your booking or after the paragliding tandem flight.
